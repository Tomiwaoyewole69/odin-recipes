Meatloaf

Description

Meatloaf is a dish made of ground meat, usually beef, mixed with other ingredients and baked in the shape of a loaf. It's often made with breadcrumbs or other binding agents, eggs, and seasonings, and sometimes topped with a sauce. The specific ingredients and preparation can vary widely, but the basic concept remains the same: a flavorful loaf of cooked meat.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Egg
  • Onion
  • Milk
  • Bread
  • Crumbs
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Mix the loaf ingredients, then transfer to a loaf pan.
  2. Mix the sauce ingredients, then pour over the loaf.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until the meatloaf is no longer pink in the center.
